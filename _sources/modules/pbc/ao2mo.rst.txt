pbc.ao2mo --- Integral transformations for crystalline Gaussian type orbitals
*****************************************************************************

The :mod:`pbc.ao2mo` module is an analog of the :mod:`ao2mo` module but with the 
Gaussian type atomic orbitals replaced by the corresponding crystalline orbitals.
